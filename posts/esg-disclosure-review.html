ESG Disclosure Quality Review

Sustainability Reporting · Mar 2026 · Python, NLP, Tableau

Problem

Sustainability reports are getting longer, but it is still difficult to judge whether disclosures are truly comparable across firms and industries.

Data

  • Public sustainability report text across sectors
  • Topic-based ESG keyword dictionaries
  • Basic firm-level financial indicators

Approach

I scored disclosure quality using topic coverage and the presence of quantitative evidence. Context-aware filtering was applied to reduce inflation from simple keyword frequency counts.

Findings

Environmental sections had relatively stronger quantitative reporting, while governance disclosures were more narrative-heavy and less comparable. Clear differences in disclosure balance also appeared by industry.

Next Step

Next, I will track year-over-year disclosure-quality improvement and connect the score with external ESG ratings for correlation analysis.
