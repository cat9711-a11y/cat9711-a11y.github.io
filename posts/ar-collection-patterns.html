AR Collection Pattern Analysis

Working Capital · Mar 2026 · SQL, Python

Problem

Delayed collections kept recurring, but action priorities were mostly based on individual experience rather than a consistent data-driven method.

Data

  • Invoice-level receivables and payment transactions
  • Customer segment, region, and payment-term attributes
  • Historical collection activity logs

Approach

I built monthly cohorts and compared DSO trajectories across segments. Then I combined overdue recurrence and exposure size into a priority score for collection follow-up.

Findings

High-risk accounts were concentrated in specific term-industry combinations. The analysis identified a group requiring earlier intervention than the standard reminder cycle.

Next Step

The next iteration will add CRM touchpoint history to estimate collection probability by contact timing and automate action guidance for the ops team.
