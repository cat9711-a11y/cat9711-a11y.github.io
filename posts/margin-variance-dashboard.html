Gross Margin Variance Dashboard

FP&A - Feb 2026 - Power BI, DAX, Excel

Problem

Margin changes were explained manually every month, which led to inconsistent narratives across teams. We needed a shared and transparent variance framework.

Data

  • Monthly sales and COGS by product line
  • Price list revisions and discount logs
  • Budget and prior-year baselines

Approach

I decomposed margin movement into price, volume, mix, and cost effects and implemented drill-down views for both management summary and analyst detail.

Findings

One product group showed positive pricing impact, but rising input cost offset most of the gain. This helped teams focus discussion on the true driver instead of top-line assumptions.

Next Step

I plan to integrate FX and promotion-calendar variables to support an early-warning view at the beginning of each month.
