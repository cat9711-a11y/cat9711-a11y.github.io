Journal Entry Risk Screening

Audit Analytics - Jan 2026 - SQL, Python

Problem

Reviewing every journal entry manually is not practical. We needed a clear risk score to prioritize entries that deserved audit attention first.

Data

  • General ledger journal entry export (12 months)
  • User ID, posting timestamp, amount, and account combinations
  • Manual adjustment flags and period-end indicators

Approach

I combined weighted signals such as unusually large amounts, weekend/night postings, rare account pairs, and month-end concentration. Thresholds were calibrated through sample-based review.

Findings

Roughly 4% of entries were classified as high-risk, and this group showed a significantly higher share of manual adjustments. The model provided a much clearer starting point for audit testing.

Next Step

Next, I plan to refresh weights quarterly and incorporate business-unit approval-policy differences to further reduce false positives.
